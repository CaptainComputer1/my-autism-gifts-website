A Few Things People Often Ask

Do I need a formal autism diagnosis to work with Rob?
No. Rob works with anyone who identifies as autistic, neurodivergent, or simply someone who wants to grow. A diagnosis is never required to access coaching.
What happens during the free consultation?
It's a relaxed 30-minute conversation — not a sales call. You share a little about where you are and what you're working toward, Rob shares how he works, and you both decide together whether it's a good fit. No pressure either way.
Can I meet in person, or is it all online?
Both options are available. If you're in the Los Angeles area, you can choose to meet in person. All sessions are also available online via Zoom — so geography is never a barrier.
How soon will Rob get back to me?
Usually within one business day for emails and voicemails. The consultation form goes directly to Rob — he reviews every submission personally.
Is coaching the right fit for me right now?
If you're wondering whether coaching is right for you — that's exactly what the free consultation is designed to answer. You don't need to be ready or certain. Just curious is enough.
