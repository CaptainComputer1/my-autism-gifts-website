About These Rates

Why These Prices?

Specialised neurodivergent coaching typically runs $145–$225 per hour. I've kept my rates intentionally accessible because I want real people — not just well-funded ones — to have access to coaching that works.

Consider the cost of not investing: missed employment opportunities, ongoing dependence, relationships that don't reach their potential. My clients regularly tell me that what they gained is worth far more than what they paid.

If cost is a barrier, let's talk. I'd rather find a way to work with you than have money be the reason you don't get support.
